Favorite and Least Favorite

Foods

#1

Fruit

My #1 favorite food is definitely a broad choice. However, this does not mean every single fruit is my favorite food; there are definitely some fruits that I do not like. Despite that, I still put fruit at the top of my favorite foods list, because most fruit is really tasty.

In case you don't know what fruit is, according to Google, fruit is the sweet and fleshy product of a tree or other plant that contains seed and can be eaten as food.

Fun Fact!

Bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't.

Joke

Q: Why did the orange stop?
A: Because it ran out of juice!

#2

Peanuts

Peanuts are my second favorite food. Unlike fruit, this one is not so broad. If I am in the mood for something salty, I'll pick salty peanuts. If I would rather have something sweet, then I'd pick honey-roasted peanuts. I love that Five Guys and Texas Roadhouse have boxes and barrels of peanuts that are completely free. I know they give them out so they can sell more drinks, but that doesn't matter cause they still taste great.

In case you don't know what peanuts are, according to Google, peanuts are the oval seed of a South American plant, widely roasted and salted and eaten as a snack.

Fun Fact!

Peanuts are the seventh most valuable crop in the US. As they should be!

Joke

Q: What do you call a peanut with a cold?
A: Cashew!

#3

Crab

Yes, Crab is my third favorite food. I know crab is rather pricey, and that is why I rarely ever have it. Crab was much cheaper where we used to live in Virginia, which is where I learned to like it. However, I am not one of those people who dip their crab in butter. That's a bit too odd for me.

In case you don't know what a crab is, according to Google, a crab is a crustacean with a broad carapace, stalked eyes, and five pairs of legs, the first pair of which are modified as pincers. Crabs are abundant on many shores, especially in the tropics, where some have become adapted to life on land.

Fun Fact!

Crabs can walk in all directions, but mostly walk and run sideways.

Joke

Q: What do you call the greatest crab artist that ever lived?
A: Leonardo da Pinci!
