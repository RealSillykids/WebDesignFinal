  • My First Animations

    Feb 5, 2019

    2019 was the year I first started creating 2D animations. Since I was just starting out, I did not care too much about the quality of the animations. My goal was to create an animation that looked decent, while focusing more on creating them quickly rather than their overall quality.

    I created four animations in 2019, with one of them being a project for school. People seemed to enjoy them, and I enjoyed making them, so I kept creating them.

  • 2020 Animations

    2020

    In 2020, I continued to create animations the same way I had been doing in 2019, creating a total of three animations. Once again, one of them was for a school project.

    However, 2020 was also the year I decided I wanted to focus on the quality of my animations, rather than quantity. After making this decision, I animated a quick lip-sync test in order to test out methods of creating higher-quality animations.

    When deciding to niche down my main channel, I created a separate channel known as "Sillykids Animations", which is where all my animations are now uploaded.
