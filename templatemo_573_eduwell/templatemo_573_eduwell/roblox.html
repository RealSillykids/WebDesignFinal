My History on Roblox

Roblox

2012-2013

frogbiscuit

When visiting some friends for Sunday brunch on March 18th, 2012, I was introduced to Roblox, an online game that resembled LEGO bricks at the time. Roblox allowed you to create your own games, and play games created by others, which I thought was really cool. When visiting our friends, I played as a guest, which allowed me to play Roblox without an account.

After we got home, my mom helped me create my first Roblox account, known as frogbiscuit. I would continue to use this account until early 2013 when my mom banned me from playing Roblox after they introduced the chat feature to users under 13.

Favorite Games

Fall Down the Stairs

This game is really simple, fall down the stairs to earn points

This is the first Roblox game I ever played because my friend told me I had to start with an "easy game."

Wave Pool Survival!

You and everyone else are spawned in a random location each round. The goal is to climb as high as you can, in order to survive the giant tsunami waves coming your way.  

2013-2018

sillykidsiscool

Although we were banned from playing, my brother Liam and I still thought about Roblox a lot. On November 13th, 2013, we gathered enough courage to ask our mom if she would let us play Roblox again. To our surprise, she said yes, and we created new accounts since we had forgotten the passwords to our original accounts.

As time went on, I eventually remembered the password to my frogbiscuit account but continued to use sillykidsiscool since I had more progress on it.

Favorite Games

Hide and Seek 

Hide and Seek by lando64000 is one of the most classic hide-and-seek games on Roblox. At the start of each round, everyone is placed on one of the 49 fields. The player who is chosen to be the IT must find everyone before the time runs out.

Deathrun

At the start of each round, one player is chosen to be the killer. The killer must use various traps to make everyone fall. Everyone else must make it to the end of the randomly chosen deathrun without falling for the killer's traps.

2018-2021

RealSillykids

On May 5th, 2016, my brother Liam and I got permission from our mom to create YouTube channels. We wanted to upload Roblox videos, but our computers were not powerful enough. Once the school year started, we got new computers and uploaded our first Roblox videos on September 1st, 2016.

After uploading Roblox videos for a while, I decided I wanted to change my username. After saving up 1000 Robux (which is what it costs to change your username), I decided to pick the name "RealSillykids", which is still my username to this day.

Favorite Games

Flee the Facility

At the start of each round, one player is chosen to be the beast, and everyone else is placed inside a random facility. The players must escape the facility before the time runs out, and the beast must capture them before they escape.

Murder Mystery 2

At the start of each round, everyone is placed on a random map. One player is selected as murderer, one as sheriff, and everyone else as innocent. The murderer must murder everyone before the time runs out, and the sheriff must stop the murderer before they kill everyone.

2021-Present

Sillykids

On June 8th, 2021, Roblox introduced the display name feature. Similar to other social media platforms, display names allowed you to have whatever name you want, even if the name was already taken by someone else.

The username "Sillykids" had been taken by some inactive account created in 2010, which is why I had settled for "RealSillykids". However, once the display name feature was released, I changed my display name to "Sillykids", allowing me to use that name on the platform, despite my username being set as "RealSillykids".

Favorite Games

Murder Mystery 2

At the start of each round, everyone is placed on a random map. One player is selected as murderer, one as sheriff, and everyone else as innocent. The murderer must murder everyone before the time runs out, and the sheriff must stop the murderer before they kill everyone.

Adopt Me!

Adopt Me! is a roleplay game where you can play as a baby or a parent. You can decorate your house, adopt and grow pets, or roleplay as a family. There is also a large trading community, where you can trade items and pets of various values with other players.
